# Campaign Launch Checklist — Quick Reference

**Before any campaign launch, verify these items:**

---

## Pre-Launch Verification

- [ ] **Conversion tracking confirmed** — test pixel fires, verify in platform dashboard
- [ ] **Landing page loads** — mobile + desktop, <2s load time
- [ ] **Keyword research complete** — high-intent keywords selected
- [ ] **Ad copy ready** — headlines ≤30 chars, descriptions ≤90 chars
- [ ] **Extensions planned** — sitelinks + callouts (15-25% CTR boost)
- [ ] **Negative keywords identified** — block junk traffic from start

---

## Platform Stabilization Windows

| Platform | Wait Before Major Changes |
|----------|---------------------------|
| Google Ads | 48-72 hours |
| Meta | 3-5 days |
| LinkedIn | 1-2 weeks |
| TikTok | 2-3 weeks |

---

## Questions to Ask Before Launch

### Budget & Strategy
1. **Daily/monthly budget?** → Sets spend ceiling
2. **Target CPA or ROAS?** → Defines success metric
3. **Campaign goal?** → Conversions, traffic, leads, awareness

### Targeting
4. **Geographic focus?** → Local, national, specific regions?
5. **Audience demographics?** → Age, income, job titles?
6. **Existing customer handling?** → Retarget or exclude?

### Creative & Landing
7. **Ad assets provided?** → Images/videos from public URL?
8. **Landing page URL?** → Must match ad promise
9. **Offer/CTA?** → What should user do next?

### Timeline & Expectations
10. **How long to evaluate?** → Minimum 2 weeks standard
11. **What constitutes success?** → X conversions, Y ROAS, Z CPA?

---

## Questions to Ask During Optimization

### Before Budget Changes
- "Can I increase budget by 20% on the winning ad group?" (with ROAS >2x data)
- "Should we scale this campaign to $X/day?" (with trend data)

### Before Pausing/Killing
- "Can I pause the underperforming ad group?" (ROAS <1x after stabilization)
- "Ready to remove this keyword?" (CPC >$3, 0 conversions, 50+ clicks)

### Before Major pivots
- "Want to test a new audience segment?" (current audience exhausted)
- "Should we try a different bidding strategy?" (not enough conversions for automation)

---

## Red Flags (Pause & Report Immediately)

| Flag | Action |
|------|--------|
| CTR <0.5% after 100 impressions | Pause ad, check relevance |
| 0 conversions after 7 days | Verify tracking first |
| CPA >3x target | Check landing page, tighten targeting |
| Quality Score <5 (Google) | Review keyword/ad copy match |

## What I Can Do Without Asking

✅ Add negative keywords (stop wasted spend)  
✅ Pause underperforming ads/keywords (<0.5% CTR)  
✅ A/B test ad copy variations  
✅ Adjust bids ±15%  
✅ Update extensions  
✅ Report performance data  

## What Requires Your Approval

❌ Activate paused campaigns  
❌ Increase budget  
❌ Resume stopped ads  
❌ Major targeting changes  
❌ New campaign launches
